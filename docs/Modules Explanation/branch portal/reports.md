# Reports

## Overview

The Reports module provides branch-level reporting and business analysis based on inventory, purchases, sales, customers, suppliers, and financial records. It helps branch users monitor operational performance and make informed business decisions.

---

## Actual Working Behavior

The Reports module retrieves business data from completed transactions and generates operational reports.

Available reports include:

- Sales reports
- Purchase reports
- Inventory reports
- Customer reports
- Supplier reports
- Financial summaries

Reports are generated using the latest available business data.

## Actual Workflow

1. User selects a report.
2. Report parameters are entered.
3. Backend retrieves required information.
4. Report calculations are performed.
5. Report is displayed for viewing or printing.

---

## Important Implementation Detail

Reports display real-time business information generated from completed operational transactions within the selected company.
