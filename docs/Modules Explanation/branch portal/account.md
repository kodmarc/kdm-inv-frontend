# Account

## Overview

The Account module manages financial records related to customers, suppliers, and business transactions. It provides users with account information required to monitor balances and financial activities within the branch.

---

## Actual Working Behavior

The Account module retrieves financial information from completed business transactions and maintains account balances automatically.

The module provides access to:

- Customer balances
- Supplier balances
- Account opening
- Financial summaries
- Outstanding balances

---

## Main Features

- Account opening
- Customer accounts
- Supplier accounts
- Balance tracking
- Financial summaries
- Account history

---

## Inputs

- Customer records
- Supplier records
- Transaction records
- Opening balances

---

## Outputs

- Updated account balances
- Financial summaries
- Outstanding balances
- Account reports

---

## Actual Workflow

1. User opens the Account module.
2. Account information is retrieved.
3. Balances are calculated.
4. Financial summaries are displayed.
5. Users can review account history and balances.

---

## Important Implementation Detail

Account balances are automatically updated after every purchase, sale, or return transaction, eliminating manual balance calculations.
